Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

Simply search for salons in your area, select your preferred service and time slot, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled time through your account dashboard or by contacting the salon directly.

How do I pay for my appointment?

Payment methods vary by salon. Most salons accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments. Payment details will be shown during the booking process.

What if I'm not satisfied with my service?

We encourage you to leave honest feedback about your experience. If you have concerns, please contact our customer support team, and we'll work with you and the salon to resolve any issues.
